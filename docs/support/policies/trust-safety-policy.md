# Trust & Safety Policy (DRAFT)

> **DRAFT — pending lawyer review (#80).**

## Summary

Rent-A-Vacation is a platform for respectful, inclusive interactions between travelers and owners. We do not tolerate discrimination, harassment, or unsafe behavior. This policy defines community standards, prohibited conduct, and enforcement.

## Details

### 1. Non-discrimination

RAV prohibits discrimination based on:

- Race, color, national origin
- Religion or creed
- Sex, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation
- Age, marital status, family status
- Disability
- Veteran or military status
- Any other category protected by applicable law

Owners cannot refuse a renter, cancel a booking, or provide a different level of service based on these categories. Renters cannot harass owners based on these categories.

### 2. Prohibited conduct

**Between users:**
- Harassment (verbal, written, sexual, or physical)
- Threats or intimidation
- Assault or battery
- Stalking
- Hate speech or symbols
- Unauthorized photography or recording in private spaces
- Sharing another user's personal information without consent

**On the platform:**
- Fraudulent listings (fake photos, misrepresenting amenities, bait-and-switch pricing)
- Fake accounts, fake reviews, manipulated ratings
- Off-platform payment demands (cash, wire transfer, gift cards, etc.)
- Circumventing platform fees
- Multiple accounts to evade bans or accumulate fake reviews
- Attempting to contact other users outside RAV communication tools with the intent to circumvent RAV
- Using the platform for illegal activity (drug manufacturing, trafficking, prostitution, etc.)

**Content:**
- Pornographic or sexually explicit listing content
- Violence, gore, or graphic content
- Spam
- Misleading statistics or health claims
- Promoting illegal substances or activities

### 3. Safety expectations

**Owners must:**
- Maintain working smoke detectors, carbon monoxide detectors, fire extinguishers
- Ensure the property meets resort/building safety standards
- Disclose known safety hazards (slippery pool deck, loose stairs, etc.) in the listing
- Provide emergency contact info at check-in
- Not install hidden cameras anywhere in the property

**Renters must:**
- Follow resort fire safety, pool safety, and other posted rules
- Not tamper with safety equipment (smoke detectors, locks)
- Report safety concerns to the owner and/or RAV immediately

### 4. Emergency situations

See [`emergency-safety-escalation.md`](../processes/emergency-safety-escalation.md) for RAV's emergency response process.

In any emergency: **call 911 (or your local emergency services) first.** RAV is a secondary channel.

### 5. Reporting violations

Users can report:

- `Report Issue` on a booking for booking-specific violations
- Email `safety@rent-a-vacation.com` for pattern-of-behavior or platform-wide concerns
- Emergency reports get < 1 hour response

Reports are confidential where possible. We may need to share some context with the reported party to investigate — this is communicated before sharing.

### 6. Enforcement

**Minor / first offense (usually content / conduct):**
- Warning
- Content removal
- Short suspension (1-7 days)

**Moderate offense:**
- Longer suspension (30 days)
- Listing removal
- Account restriction (limited to pre-launch tier features)

**Severe offense (safety, fraud, repeat offender, law-breaking):**
- Permanent platform ban
- Listing removal
- Financial clawback (funds held pending investigation)
- Law enforcement coordination
- Civil action where appropriate

**Appeals:** single appeal per enforcement action, reviewed by senior admin. Post-launch this may become a formal appeal process.

### 7. Respectful dispute process

- State facts, not personal attacks
- Provide evidence (photos, messages, timestamps)
- Be responsive when admin asks for clarification
- Misuse of the dispute flow (false claims, abusive language) is itself a violation

### 8. Community values

- Treat every user as you'd want to be treated
- Honest listings, accurate descriptions, fair prices
- Respond to messages in a timely manner (< 24h typical)
- Take care of others' property
- Keep promises — both parties

### 9. Transparency reporting

Post-launch, RAV will publish an annual transparency report including: dispute counts, enforcement actions taken, law enforcement requests received and responded to.

### 10. Cooperation with law enforcement

We respond to valid legal process. We notify affected users before disclosure where legally permitted. Emergency disclosures (imminent risk to life) may proceed without advance notice.

### 11. Contact

- Safety reports: `safety@rent-a-vacation.com`
- Bias or discrimination reports: `safety@rent-a-vacation.com` (subject "DISCRIMINATION")
- Law enforcement requests: `legal@rent-a-vacation.com`
