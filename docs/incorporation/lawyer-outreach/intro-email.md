# Intro Email — Florida Marketplace + Timeshare Counsel

> Short outreach email to Greenberg Traurig (Orlando hospitality practice) and/or Holland & Knight (vacation ownership group), per Brian T. Lower's Round 1 boardroom recommendation. Goal: secure a 30-minute initial conversation, gauge fit, and provide enough context that they can come prepared.

> **How to use:** Send to a named partner if you have a referral. If no warm intro, send to the practice's general contact email (usually on the firm's website). Keep the email under 250 words; the detailed engagement scope lives in the [RFP](rfp.md) which you attach OR send only if they express interest.

> **Context on tone:** Professional but specific. Demonstrate that you've done the homework. Lawyers screen volume; concrete questions get higher response rates than open-ended "do you handle marketplaces?"

## Sending checklist

Before sending:

- [ ] Founder term sheet signed by all 4 founders (Section 1 of the RFP references this)
- [ ] Atlas filing started (so the entity name is real, not aspirational)
- [ ] OR: explicit caveat that "Atlas filing in progress; Cert expected by [DATE]" (Yokum's Round 2 point — engagement can start before entity exists, but lawyer needs to know the entity will exist within ~2 weeks)
- [ ] Replace `[PARTNER NAME]`, `[REFERRER NAME]`, `[PHONE]`, `[EMAIL]`, `[DATE]` placeholders
- [ ] Decide whether to attach the RFP at first contact or hold for second exchange
- [ ] Mark `https://rent-a-vacation.com` is Staff Only Mode — recipient can't access; offer a walkthrough call instead
