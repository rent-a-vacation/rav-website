## Agenda

### Block 1 — Set context (5 min)

Sujit opens with a quick recap of why we're signing a term sheet:

- Stripe Atlas filing starts this week
- Atlas issues formal Stock Purchase Agreements at incorporation
- We need to agree on equity, vesting, roles, and board composition **before** clicking incorporate

Open floor for clarifying questions on the explainer. Tonight's outcome: a signed term sheet by end of meeting. Everything else flows from that.

---

### Block 2 — Equity split (30-45 min)

Run the worksheet process described in `equity-split-briefing.pdf` (reading-half) and `equity-split-worksheet-form.pdf` (fill-in-half, printed copies on the table).

**Step 1 (5-10 min) — Individual picks:**
- Each founder receives a printed copy of the worksheet form
- 10-minute timer; no discussion; phones face down
- Each founder picks A / B / C / Other, writes one-sentence reasoning, marks acceptable range
- The three options on the menu: A = 25/25/25/25 (equal split); B = 30/30/20/20 (modest weighted split); C = 35/30/20/15 (graduated split)

**Step 2 (5 min) — Reveal:**
- All four worksheets on the table at the same time
- Each founder reads out their pick + one-sentence reasoning + acceptable-range checkmarks
- Picks captured on a whiteboard or shared screen

**Step 3 (20-30 min) — Discussion:**

Three possible outcomes:

- **Everyone picked the same option:** Done in 5 minutes. Write it into the term sheet. Move to Block 3.
- **Picks differ but acceptable ranges overlap:** The overlap IS the answer. Pick from the overlap; discuss any small tweaks; lock the number.
- **Picks differ AND ranges don't overlap:** Surface which of the three "how to think about your choice" questions in the briefing we disagree on (founding equity vs other forms of recognition / binary vs graduated acknowledgment / 18-month outlook). Often the underlying disagreement is on one of those — once we align on it, the number follows.

**Group rule:** If the conversation gets stuck for >20 minutes on Step 3, we stop and schedule a follow-up within 7 days. We don't push through when everyone is tired and frustrated.

**Hard rule:** the equity discussion ends with **four numbers totaling 100% written down**. No "we'll figure out the exact percentages later."

### Block 3 — Resolve remaining term-sheet decisions (15 min)

Walk through the open decision points in the term sheet:

| Section | Decision | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Role titles | Each founder fills in the role-selection form (printed copies on the table) — 5 min. Reveal simultaneously, discuss any collisions. Self-nomination only — no founder picks anyone else's title. Non-default titles ("President", "Founder & X", "Chief Strategy Officer") are acceptable. |
| 1 | OBA status per founder | Each founder confirms status: Cleared (with employer + date) / Pending (with date filed + expected resolution) / Not yet filed |
| 2 | Founder allocation % | Pick from 70-80% range (recommend 75% — middle of standard range) |
| 2 | Option pool % | Pick from 10-15% range (recommend 10% — conservative for a 4-founder pre-launch company) |
| 5 | OBA reservation timeframe | Pick from 6/12/18 months (recommend 12 — gives realistic time for the OBA process while preventing indefinite reservation) |
| 7 | Board composition | Decide Option A (sole director) vs Option B (two co-directors). **Constraint:** founders with pending OBA disclosures should NOT take a director seat — director status is more legally exposed than future-officer status. |

Decisions get filled in on a working copy of the term sheet as they're made.

This gives a clean audit trail and uniform signed PDF for all four founders without requiring same-room scanning.

### Block 5 — Confirm next steps (10 min)

What happens after signing:

1. **Sujit:** Starts Atlas filing the same week. Atlas takes 7-10 business days for Cert + EIN + Stripe live + Mercury bank.
2. **OBA-cleared Founders:** Be ready to sign formal Stock Purchase Agreements when Atlas generates them (within 2 weeks).
3. **OBA-cleared Founders:** Mail 83(b) elections within 30 days of receiving stock. **HARD IRS DEADLINE** — set calendar reminders right now in the room.
4. **OBA-pending Founders:** Continue OBA disclosure process. Notify other founders the moment OBA clears so we can issue your stock + file 83(b) within 30 days of clearance.
5. **All four:** Sign IP assignment agreements (CIIAAs) at incorporation. These are separate from the Stock Purchase Agreements and apply regardless of OBA status.
6. **Sujit:** Sends the lawyer outreach intro emails this week (separate workstream).
7. **OBA-cleared Founders:** Begin proactive brand-protection outreach to Hilton Grand Vacations / Marriott Vacations Worldwide / Disney Vacation Club within 30 days of incorporation.
