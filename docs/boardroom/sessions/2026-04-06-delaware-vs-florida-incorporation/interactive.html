Founder Briefing

The verdict is strong, not narrow. Five advisors with different lenses converged on Delaware C-Corp + Florida foreign entity registration for five non-overlapping reasons. When that kind of convergence happens, the Florida-only path looks attractive from inside the founding team for legitimate reasons but is paid for by friction at every downstream inflection point.

The core tension was whether the charter decision substitutes for substantive Florida regulatory work. The room committed to treating these as parallel non-negotiable tracks, which moved Brian and Jason from CONDITIONAL to YES.

The sharpest insight came from Brian's Round 2 reversal: corporate structure is not just an M&A optimization — it changes whether resort brand protection counsel start the conversation with you with a phone call or a cease-and-desist letter.
