## Biggest Fight

The core tension was whether Delaware C-Corp delivers anything beyond "acquisition-readiness optionality" (Brian's initial pushback against Yokum). The room resolved it by demonstrating Delaware delivers value across five non-overlapping dimensions: M&A optionality (Yokum), founder-dispute predictability and litigation defense alignment (Tonia), payments-ecosystem default-fit (Patrick), industry-peer signaling to resort brands (Jason), and — the surprise — actual operational change in how brand-protection counsel at Marriott/Hilton/Wyndham/Disney respond when they first notice the platform (Brian, from 26 years inside HICV's brand protection function).

## Sharpest Insight

> "The first kind of platform got a phone call. The second kind got a cease-and-desist letter." — Brian Lower, on how resort brand protection counsel actually treat Delaware C-Corps versus Florida LLCs in the first conversation.
