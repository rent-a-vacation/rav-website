# Social Media Strategy — Rent-A-Vacation

| Platform | Account Cost | Paid Features (Optional) |
|----------|-------------|-------------------------|
| Google Business Profile | **FREE** | Google Ads (pay-per-click, $5+/day) |

| Facebook Business Page | **FREE** | Meta Ads ($5+/day), page verification |

## Platform Priority & Audience Match

| Priority | Platform | Primary Audience | Content Type | Frequency |
|----------|----------|-----------------|--------------|-----------|
| 1 | **Google Business Profile** | Everyone (search) | Info, reviews, posts | 1/week |
| 2 | **LinkedIn** | VCs, partners, B2B | Thought leadership, milestones | 2/week |
| 3 | **Facebook** | Timeshare owners (45-65) | Education, community, deals | 3/week |
| 4 | **Instagram** | Travelers (28-55) | Resort photos, Reels, Stories | 3/week + 5 stories |
| 5 | **X/Twitter** | Tech/startup community | News, tips, quick updates | 3-5/week |

## Content Pillars (Recurring Themes)

1. **Resort Showcases** — Beautiful property photos with savings breakdown
2. **Owner Success** — "How I offset my maintenance fees" stories
3. **Travel Deals** — Specific savings examples, seasonal promotions
4. **Behind the Build** — Tech updates, AI demos, product milestones
5. **Industry Insights** — Timeshare market data, travel trends
6. **Tool Spotlights** — SmartEarn, SmartCompare, SmartMatch features

## Launch Sequence

### Week 1: Account Setup
1. Create all 5 accounts with consistent branding (see platform content files)
2. Upload logos, cover photos, bios
3. Add website links to all profiles
4. Update website footer with social links

### Week 2: Seed Content
1. Publish 3-5 posts per platform (pre-written content in this folder)
2. Pin launch announcement on each platform
3. Cross-link all profiles

### Week 3+: Active Posting
1. Follow the content calendar (see below)
2. Engage with comments and messages
3. Join relevant Facebook Groups and LinkedIn communities

## Long-Term Marketing Automation

### Free Tools (Start Here)

| Tool | What It Does | Cost | Use For |
|------|-------------|------|---------|
| **Meta Business Suite** | Schedule Facebook + Instagram posts, view analytics | Free | Core scheduling for FB/IG |
| **LinkedIn native scheduler** | Schedule LinkedIn posts up to 3 months ahead | Free | LinkedIn posting |

### Low-Cost Tools (When Ready to Scale)

| Tool | What It Does | Cost | When to Add |
|------|-------------|------|-------------|
| **Buffer** | Schedule across 3 channels, analytics | Free (3 channels) / $6/mo (unlimited) | When posting > 3x/week |
| **Later** | Visual Instagram planner, link-in-bio | Free (30 posts/mo) / $25/mo | When IG becomes primary |
| **Hootsuite** | Full social management, team collaboration | $99/mo | When team grows to 3+ |
| **Mailchimp** | Email marketing, drip campaigns | Free (500 contacts) | When building email list |
| **Zapier** | Automate: new listing -> social post | Free (100 tasks/mo) | When automating workflows |

### Growth Phase Automation (Post-Launch)

When the platform has active users and listings, automate content creation:

1. **New listing -> Auto-post**: When an owner lists a property, auto-generate a social post with property photo, resort name, and nightly rate. Tools: Zapier + Buffer API.

2. **Deal alerts -> Social posts**: When a listing is priced significantly below market (SmartPrice data), auto-post a "deal alert" to Facebook/Instagram.

3. **Milestone posts**: Auto-generate when hitting milestones (100 listings, 1000 users, first booking, etc.).

4. **User-generated content**: Encourage guests to share vacation photos with #RentAVacation — reshare the best ones.

5. **Email -> Social pipeline**: Repurpose email newsletter content as social posts (one piece of content, multiple formats).
