## Community Building

### Facebook Groups to Join (for organic engagement)
Search for and join these types of groups:
- "Timeshare Owners" groups (multiple exist, 5K-50K members)
- "[Brand] Owners" groups (e.g., "Hilton Grand Vacations Owners")
- "Timeshare Rentals and Resales" groups
- "[Destination] Vacation Rentals" groups
- "Timeshare Exit" groups (owners looking for alternatives — our target!)

**Engagement rules:** Don't spam. Provide value first. Answer questions. Share the SmartEarn calculator as a helpful tool when relevant. Let people come to you.

### Optional: Create "RAV Owners Community" Group
A private group for RAV property owners to:
- Share tips on listing optimization
- Discuss pricing strategies
- Connect with other owners
- Get early access to new features
